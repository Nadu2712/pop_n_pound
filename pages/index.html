Game Rules

  • Each click on a banana gives 10 points
  • Multiple clicks on the same banana are allowed to accumulate more points
  • Clicking on a monkey costs 1 life
  • Players lose a life for each monkey clicked
  • Players start with 3 lives
  • After losing all 3 lives, the game pauses, and the player gets a chance to continue or end
  • To continue the game, the player must answer a quiz question correctly
  • Players are given 3 attempts to answer the question
  • The appearance speed of bananas and monkeys increases every 60 seconds
